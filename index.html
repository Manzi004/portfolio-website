About Me

Hi! I'm a junior software developer with a passion for creating applications that solve real-world problems. After graduating from Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada with a degree in Computer Programming, my journey in tech has been driven by my love for using modern technologies to tackle everyday challenges.

What initially drew me to programming was the ability to solve real-world issues through code – there's something incredibly satisfying about turning a complex problem into an elegant solution that people can actually use and benefit from.

I specialize in full-stack development, working with technologies like React, Angular, Spring Boot, Node.js, and various database systems. I'm particularly passionate about Spring Boot development, and you'll often find me working in IntelliJ with MySQL databases. What I find most rewarding about development is the process of taking an idea from concept to a working application that users can actually benefit from.

When I'm not coding, you can find me gaming, building PCs, or watching basketball and football games. I believe these hobbies actually complement my development skills – gaming keeps me thinking about user experience, and building PCs gives me a deeper appreciation for how hardware and software work together.

Based in Ottawa, Canada, I'm open to remote opportunities and freelance projects. Let's connect and discuss how we can work together! Feel free to reach out to discuss opportunities – I'm always happy to chat about technology and development.
